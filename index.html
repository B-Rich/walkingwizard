ABOUT

We put together this terminal walking wizard that pins down your walking style and recommends a concrete, actionable behavior you can do today. We're engineers and behavior designers at Stanford who believe us makers should walk once in a while to exercise, take a break from machines, or just get around town. Make and walk. Repeat. Enjoy!
